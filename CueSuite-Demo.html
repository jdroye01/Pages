Feature Overview

Everything a sound op
actually needs.

Built for the real demands of live theatre, worship, and events — not a music player with extra steps.

🎛️
Grid Layout
Up to dozens of cues per page, arranged in a customisable drag-and-drop grid. Pages expand automatically as you add cues.
iPad
📍
Named Markers
Mark any moment inside a cue — verse, chorus, sfx sting, scene change. Jump there instantly with one tap from the iPad or iPhone.
iPad · Mobile
🔗
Next Cue Chaining
When a cue ends, the next one starts automatically. Chain to a full cue or directly to a specific marker within it.
iPad
📱
Mobile Remote
CueSuite Mobile on iPhone gives the sound operator full wireless control — over Wi-Fi or Bluetooth — from anywhere in the venue.
iPhone
🔊
Fade Out
Smooth one-second fade triggered from the iPad or remotely from iPhone. No abrupt cuts mid-scene.
iPad · Mobile
🔐
Password Protection
Lock remote control behind a password so only your designated operator can send commands from the iPhone.
Security
🎵
Any Audio Format
MP3, M4A, WAV, FLAC, AIFF, CAF, WMA. Files from the Files app or Apple Music library — DRM-free tracks get full volume and marker control.
Audio
🔇
Ignores Silent Switch
Audio routes through the speaker regardless of the hardware silent switch — critical for live performance reliability.
Reliability
🔒
Per-Cue Volume Lock
Set the exact volume for each cue and lock it to prevent accidental changes during a show. Unlock with a single tap when adjustments are needed.
iPad
